Son Manh Nguyen | 원맹산

Hi, I'm Son (Sonn), a university student passionate about using artificial intelligence (AI) in drug discovery and development from natural compounds.

Currently, I am working at the Organic Biochemistry Laboratory of the Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology, where I am conducting scientific research in the field of natural compounds chemistry.

Previously, I worked at the Center for Computational Chemistry at the Vietnam National University - University of Science, where I learned and researched computational methods in chemistry.

My current personal research project is to explore the use of artificial neural networks to investigate the chemical properties and toxicity of semi-synthetic compounds from natural compounds.
